AI agent suite

      
Many agents, one governed clinical workflow.

      

        
01
Voice Intake Agent
Captures spoken notes and places facts into the right form fields.

        
02
Session Note Agent
Drafts structured therapy notes from raw staff entries for review.

        
03
Behavior ABC Agent
Extracts antecedent, behavior, consequence, intensity, duration, location, and suspected function.

        
04
Plan Assistant
Turns BCBA guidance into draft goals, interventions, measurement criteria, and review reminders.

        
05
QA & Compliance Agent
Flags missing signatures, late notes, gaps in required fields, and inconsistent billing support.

        
06
Report Writer
Drafts monthly progress, incident summaries, and caregiver/provider reports with source traceability.

      

    


    

      
Compliance posture

      
Built for HIPAA-conscious operations, not casual note-taking.

      

        
MFA requiredAll users accessing PHI must enable MFA before production access.

        
Tenant isolationEvery query is scoped by organization ID and role.

        
Audit loggingPatient access, note creation, AI draft generation, and admin actions are logged.

        
Minimum necessaryRoles expose only the records and workflows required for the user’s job.

        
AI safety gatePHI is not sent to an external LLM unless a BAA/Healthcare Addendum is signed and explicitly enabled.

        
Human approvalAI drafts are never final clinical records until reviewed and signed by an authorized clinician.
